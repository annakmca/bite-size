Change and Habits

Self-Regulation

Although self-regulation is not a concept that we talk about very often today, I believe that is changing, and it will be something you hear about more and more. Self-regulation has 2 parts: first awareness of our thoughts and mental state, and second finding ways to spend more time in optimal mental states that allow us to feel calmer, more productive, and more open to the experiences and opportunities around us.

Habits and Routines

Our routines and habits are the small actions that build who we are and what we accomplish. When we break our actions and routines down into small, manageable steps that we are excited about then we are the most likely to accomplish them and keep doing them in the future as well.

Organization and Soul-Searching

I’ve found that when our mind and our spaces are organized in a way that works for us, we are more able to be open and creative. But what feels good to one person doesn't work well for another. We need to learn to connect to what works best for us individually in all aspects of our lives.
